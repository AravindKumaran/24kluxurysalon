Luxury Hair Wash Treatments

Use lukewarm water
Really hot water might seem relaxing but it’s definitely not good for your strands. Keep the temperature on the cooler side. This will help seal in the moisture and help improve the texture of your hair in the long run.
Don’t scrub your scalp
A good massage to dislodge all the grime and dirt is good but damaging your scalp with your nails isn’t. Be gentle and massage your head in circular motions using the pads of your fingers.
Wash twice
It turns out that the rinse-lather-and-repeat formula actually works. The first round removes any build-up while the second cleanses your scalp.
Time your conditioner
Yes, it’s vital to use one and it’s also important to give your conditioner time to really nourish your strands but don’t leave it on your hair for more than five minutes. It can leave residue in your hair that will take away the shine.
Rinse, rinse, rinse
You know how long they spend rinsing your hair post washing and conditioning it at the salon? This is to ensure that you get out all traces of product in your hair as this will cause it to be dull and lackluster.
Treat your hair gently after washing
Don’t vigorously towel-dry your hair post washing with a towel. Instead, gently dry your strands and use a heat protectant before styling
